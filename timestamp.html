Unix Time Reference
Unix time = seconds since 1 Jan 1970 00:00:00 UTC (the Unix epoch).
Milliseconds = Unix × 1000. JavaScript's Date.now() returns milliseconds.
The Year 2038 problem: 32-bit signed integers overflow at 2,147,483,647 (19 Jan 2038 03:14:07 UTC).
ISO 8601 format: YYYY-MM-DDTHH:mm:ssZ — the Z means UTC.
